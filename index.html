Rajdeep is academically from Computer Application background with a post-graduation degree and currently working as an Oracle Apps Professional. Beside of his professional profile, he is a technical blogger since 2017. Till now he has published his blog/articles on different platforms including WordPress, Medium, Quora, Tumblr, IndiBlogger and also periodically writes on Edutech portals like GeeksforGeeks & many more. According to IndiBlogger.in, he is counted among the top 10 cyber-security, top 200 education, top 100 WordPress tagged bloggers in India with a phenomenal overnight reach across social medias since 2018. In GeeksforGeeks Technical Scripter 2019 Anuual Event he was declared among Top 50 technical content writers with 44th national rank. Apparently he worked for many top Indian Edutech industries either as a campus-based or local chapter-based tech lead or as an event-specific mentor or as a sessional webinar-host or as a community lead in a digital marketing team and even worked in the role of a digital content developer either as a writer or graphics designer or web designer.

Alongside the journey of blogger and tech-lead, he has a vast interest in Machine Learning & Cloud computing also. He has worked on few projects on Natural Language Processing while he has also lined up serial records of achievements & hall of fames in cloud computing and cyber-security respectively.

Early life & education

Rajdeep was born on 3rd June, 1997 in Kolkata to Mr. Bikash Ranjan Das who is a retired West Bengal Govt. staff & Mrs. Rita Das who is a housewife. Rajdeep is belonging from a Bengali middle class family residing at Jadavpur.

He has completed his schooling including both 10th (Y. 2014) & 12th (Y. 2016) standard from Jadavpur Vidyapith (Kolkata) & graduated in Bachelor of Computer Application from Netaji Subhash Engineering College in 2016. Later he has completed his post-graduation in Master of Computer Application from Heritage Institute of Technology-Kolkata in 2022.

Rajdeep has also completed his "Chitra Bhushan" degree & partially "Chitra Visharad" in Fine Arts (Painting) upto 4th year in 2012 under Pracheen Kala Kendra, Chandigarh.

During post-graduation, Rajdeep has done few globlly accredited professional certification beside his academic curriculum including:

  • Google certified in Google Cloud Platform Business Professional.
  • International Cyber Security Institute (ICSI) certified in CNSS Professional (Certified Network Security Specialist).
  • Fortinet Security certified in Network Security Expert Associate level 1, level 2 & level 3 (NSE1, NSE2 & NSE3) respectively.
  • IBM certified in IT Security fundamentals.
  • Amazon Web Service (AWS) certified in Machine Learning Terminology.
  • ISO/IEC 27001 Information Security Associate certification in Information Security Management System (ISMS) via SkillFront.
  • ISO/IEC 20000 IT Service Management Associate certification inInformation Technology Infrastructure Library (ITIL) via SKillFront.
  • Six Sigma White, Green, Black Belt Professional via Council for Six Sigma Certification Association + GreyCampus + 6sigma.
  • E-Marketing Institute certified in Search Engine Optiimization Speciaization & Blogging.
  • DeepLearning.ai certified in Machine Learning & Deep Learning specialization.

Career

Rajdeep has completed his internship at Cognizant Technology Solutions in the Oracle Solution Practice domain in July, 2022 and in August 2022 he joined there as a full time employee in EAS Oracle Apps Solution Developer role. Before internship at Cognizant, Rajdeep has done few remotely held training & internship programs at several oraganizations/startup companies and serially earned letter of recommendations, certificate of recognitions & letter of appreciations.

During his graduation period, Rajdeep first started a website with the name "WebTex" by taking a third party free hosting service in 2017 to provide free learning resources on Web Designing & Developing especially for school children & beginners by collecting study materials from different other resources at the same place. The idea worked fine initially but ultimately didn't reach the expectation & just in a few months due to lack of traffics the website got closed permanently.

Starting of blogging

In the same year, Rajdeep started his personal blog on WordPress.com, mostly on cyber-security, web technology and a little bit about trending techn-news. The blog started grabbing attention from the people of same blogging commnunity on WordPress.com. Seeing his writings were getting huge responses, the next idea was inherited to start an official blog seperatedly from his personal blog. In February, 2018 he launched his first official blog with a brand name "TheTechLearner" on the WordPress subdomain.

TheTechLearner was initially focused on sharing cyber-news, trending technology updates but unfortuanately it was not working on the ground as expected. Because of his same taste of technical contents had already made a position through his personal blog, so his officially 'branded' blog was not getting the same responses.

Things were started changing just after a few months when he published a special series of blogs with the title "How to start a blog" which was intended to guide newbiews who are equally interested to start their own blog. This series of blogs was included with not only the technical aspects of blogging but also covering the necessity to learn the importance of organic growth hack. After having a long way of sharing the series in threads across different social media handles fequently, finally it got its deserving outcome within 3 months and gained 1M+ views in all over.

The next thing was happened when he started answering to the questions regarding blogging tips and solutions asked by the aspirant bloggers on Quora.com. Quora was his first and the smartest way to promote TheTechLearner and its series of blogs by writing answers on the same niche he usually does on his series and then backliking his relevant blogs in each of those answers. He got 60K+ views in those 100+ question-answerings.

In between that he got in touch with several other third party ad & marketing camps and his blogs got more & more promotions & in 2019 he became the part of IndiBlogger: India's Biggest blog directly and the largest blogging community with most active bloggers in India. Rajdeep promoted both of his blogs (personal + TheTechLearner) on IndiBlogger & shared with other bloggers. Both of the blogs were ranked 72 and 54 respetively.

According to IndiBlogger's most demanded popular blog-directory statistics since 2019, Rajdeep was listed among Top 10 Cyber Security bloggers, Top 200 Education bloggers, Top 100 WordPress bloggers. In the same year, Rajdeep was in the list Top 44th technical content writers in Expert-voted category in GeeksforGeeks Technical Scripter event- A national level event and in that same event of 2020 he was among Top 100 in Hard-voted category.

In 2019, Rajdeep created another sub-blog (child-blog) named HackiTalki-WikiWeb while keeping TheTechLearner as parent blog on the WordPress subdomain.

HackiTalki-WikiWeb is totally focused on sharing CyberTech news & Cyber Crime + Cyber Security Awareness program.

Rajdeep also started a technews portal and tech-quiz portals in both of his blogs and it was apparently successful on social platforms. Since 2019, Rajdeep was not so able to frequently publish blogs due to his academic and career-pressure but he has made a strong presence of his blogs on soial media platforms with massive response.

In 2021, Rajdeep created two open source forum on GitHub Organization profiles in the name of his blogs where any people can contribute open source projects or ideas which was considered to be a great movement for open source enthusiasts.

By the end of 2020, both of his blogs were profiled on Crunchbase public forum with popular choice blogs in India.

From 2019 to 2021 he consistently wrote many technical and digital marketing related articles, case studies including guest blogging, freelancing for writing industries, event based competitive writing & few research journals in the name of edutech industries. He has also published technical reports & presentations on both ResearchGate & ResearchID respectively.

As a speaker/host

In 2020 Rajdeep was chosen to be Internshala Student Leader and he was in the responsibility to host three consecutive virtual career guiding webinars as a speaker. All those webinars were successful with lots of responses. The virtual webinars were on:

  1. "How to grab valid internships during pandemic 2020",
  2. "How to get trained professionally with on-demand technical skills"
  3. "Talk-Fest on technical and non-technical skill growth tips and tracks".

He was also the keynote speaker for GirlScript Foundation's most populerly held Open-Source Contribution Program Seminar (2021) and Coding Block & Coding Ninjas' session learning speaker (2020-2021). In the same year, he was a guest speaker at a Cyber Awareness virtual conference organized by MAKAUT engineering students in a private engineering College.

As an open source contributor and mentor

Since 2018, Rajdeep started contributing in open source project in several events, sponsored by different organizations. The most engaged one among all was GirlScript Foundation. After starting an open source contribution journey from there, Rajdeep has serially achieved lots of recognitions and award certificates in many Open Source contribution programs either as a direct contributor (in Team or as individual) or as a mentor or as a project admin or as a open source program supervisor. He has made a serially lined up list of 20+ successful open source contribution and mentorship programs including Script Foundation (Now Social) Winter of Code, GirlScript Summer of Code 2018-2022 (Total 5 times in all the roles over the 5 years including Contributor, Mentor, Supervisor, Project Admin and Team Lead), GirlScript Winter of Code, Social Summer of Code, Delhi Technological University IEEE Cross Winter of Code, Jalpaiguri Government Engineering College Winter of Code, NIT Trichy Delta Force Winter of Code, DevIncept Winter of Code, Google Developer Student Club Netaji Subhash Engineering College Winter of Code, IIT Kharagpur Winter of Code, GeeksforGeeks Game of Source, Digital Ocean and Intel Corporation held Hacktoberfest, LetsGrowMore Summer of Code, DevScript Winter of Code, IIT Patna NJack Winter of Code, Hackclub SAL Open Source Program, Twowaits Hacktoberfest, AppWrite Hacktoberfest, RedWoodJS Open Source Contribution Program, CodXCrypt Open Source Contribution Program, Microsoft Student Technical Club Winter of Code 4.0 DA-IICT Open Source Contribution Program.

Moreover, he contributed to the organization projects of IIIT Kottayam, Google Developer Student Club RCC Institute of Technology, Fueler.io, CommunityPro club, BitProject, GitHub Education program via GitHub. He is also available on GitLab where he has contributed to few mini projects for student learning resources. Rajdeep was also selected in the GitHub Graduation Program 2021 and he has also achieved Major League Hacking Fellowship Open Source program on GitHub.

Collaboration with Google Cloud Career Readiness Program

In 2021, Rajdeep was a participant in Google Cloud Career Readiness Competition where some Google Cloud tracks were given to finish to touch 3 specific milestones as achievement. Due to academic pressure he was not able to finish all but achieved Milestone 1 and Milestone 2 respectiely. Therefore he was also got another chance to become Google Cloud Chennai campus outreach for Google Cloud Community Days India.
